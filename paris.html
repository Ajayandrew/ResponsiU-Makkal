L'Art de Vivre

Experience the Romance of France

From the iconic iron silhouette of the Eiffel Tower to the cobblestone streets of Montmartre, Paris is an open-air museum. Dive into world-class art, unmatched gastronomy, and centuries of revolutionary history in the heart of France.

La Tour Eiffel

The global symbol of France. Whether viewed from the Trocadéro at sunrise or sparkling with lights at night, it remains the city's most breathtaking marvel.

Musée du Louvre

The world's largest art museum and a historic monument. Home to the Mona Lisa and the Venus de Milo, housed within a magnificent former royal palace.

Notre-Dame de Paris

A masterpiece of French Gothic architecture. Standing on the Île de la Cité, its towers and stained glass have watched over the city for centuries.

Sacré-Cœur & Montmartre

Perched on a hill, this white basilica offers the best panoramic views of Paris. Explore the winding alleys where Picasso and Dalí once lived.
