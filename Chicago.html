CHICAGO

Architecture & Innovation

The Heart of the Midwest

From the world-class skyscrapers of the Loop to the shimmering shores of Lake Michigan, Chicago is a vibrant metropolis celebrated for its bold architecture, legendary jazz, and deep-dish spirit.

Millennium Park

Home to the iconic "Bean" (Cloud Gate), this public park is a masterpiece of landscape design and public art.

Willis Tower

Experience the Skydeck for unparalleled views of four states from one of the tallest buildings in the Western Hemisphere.

Navy Pier

Chicago's lakefront playground featuring the Centennial Wheel, gardens, museums, and endless entertainment.

Art Institute

One of the oldest and largest art museums in the world, housing an extraordinary collection of Impressionist and Post-Impressionist works.
