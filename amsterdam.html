Amsterdam

The Venice of the North

Welcome to the City of Canals

From its artistic heritage and elaborate canal system to its narrow houses with gabled facades, Amsterdam is one of Europe's most unique destinations. Explore the history, the tulips, and the vibrant culture of the Netherlands' capital.

The Canal Ring

A UNESCO World Heritage site, the 17th-century canal district is the heart of the city's charm. Perfect for boat tours or evening walks.

Rijksmuseum

The national museum of the Netherlands dedicated to Dutch arts and history, featuring masterpieces by Rembrandt and Vermeer.

Anne Frank House

A somber and powerful historic site where Anne Frank and her family hid during WWII. It remains one of the city's most visited landmarks.

Tulips & Gardens

Experience the iconic Dutch flower culture. While Keukenhof is a short trip away, the city's flower markets are a year-round delight.
