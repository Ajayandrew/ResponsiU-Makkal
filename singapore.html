Singapore

Where Future Meets Nature

The Garden City

Experience a world where nature and technology blend seamlessly. From the futuristic Supertrees to the heritage shophouses of Chinatown, Singapore is a dynamic multicultural hub of food, shopping, and green innovation.

Nature Park

Gardens by the Bay

Wander beneath the 50-meter-tall Supertrees and visit the world's largest glass greenhouse, featuring a man-made mountain and waterfall.

Iconic Landmark

Marina Bay Sands

The crown jewel of the skyline. Take in panoramic views from the SkyPark Observation Deck or marvel at the ship-like structure atop three towers.

Architecture

Jewel Changi

More than an airport—a destination itself. Witness the Rain Vortex, the world's tallest indoor waterfall, surrounded by a lush forest valley.

Heritage

Merlion Park

Snap a photo with Singapore's mythical mascot—half lion, half fish—spouting water into the bay against the backdrop of the city skyline.
